Key Ingredients

Science Meets Nature

Bakuchiol

Psoralea corylifolia

A plant-derived compound celebrated in modern skincare for its skin-smoothing properties. Sourced from the babchi plant, bakuchiol is known for being gentle yet effective.

Why it matters: Bakuchiol supports the look of firmer, smoother skin without the harshness sometimes associated with other ingredients. Suitable for daily use.

Shea Butter

Vitellaria paradoxa

A time-honoured, deeply nourishing butter extracted from the African shea tree. Rich in fatty acids and vitamins, it helps condition and soften skin beautifully.

Why it matters: Shea butter provides a cushion of moisture that helps skin feel supple and protected, creating a luxurious, comforting texture on the skin.

For cosmetic use. Patch test recommended before first use.
